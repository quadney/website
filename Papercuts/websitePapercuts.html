Storyline

Papercuts is the story of Ripley, a sick boy with a vivid imagination, and his paper creation - Rip, the boy with a box for a head.

One night when Ripley's sickness is particularly painful, Rip comes to life in his dream, except this dream is more of a nightmare! In this paper world dream, the evil Paper Witch has taken Ripley's parents, dog, and soon-to-be-friend.

Play as Rip to cut and snip your way through Ripley's house with the help of your trusty scissors, in order to save the boy's loved one's, and ultimately yourself.

Demo Day

Papercuts was presented to the public on April 21, 2013 in the Computer Science and Engineering Building at the University of Florida, as part of "CISE Day". Many other projects were presented, such as other games from our class, Theory and Production of Digital Arts and Sciences, and AI for Computer Games.

As user's approached Papercuts, they would play the game inside the head of Rip, and the first 10 players to finish the game received a hand-printed Papercuts t-shirt.
